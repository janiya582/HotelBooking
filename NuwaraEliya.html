Heritance Tea Factory

( Kandapola, Nuwara Eliya 22220, Sri Lanka)

Perched at 6,800 feet above sea level, Heritance Tea Factory combines historic charm and luxury, allowing guests to experience Sri Lanka’s tea heritage in a repurposed tea factory. This unique property offers modern comfort within a preserved colonial structure, perfect for those seeking a blend of culture, history, and breathtaking scenery. Once an active tea factory, this hotel offers a unique experience in the heart of Sri Lanka’s tea country. Rooms retain the industrial heritage of the factory with elegant touches and expansive views of the surrounding plantations. The on-site restaurant provides a historic dining experience, using original factory equipment as decor, while the tea-themed spa treatments and guided tours let guests fully immerse themselves in Sri Lanka’s tea culture.

Room Categories:
  • Superior Rooms: Comfortable, cozy rooms with plantation views and classic decor.
  • Executive Floor Rooms: Spacious rooms with additional amenities and exclusive views of the hills.
  • Flower Dew Suite: Luxurious suite with a separate living area, premium furnishings, and panoramic views.
Rates and Pricing:

Starting from $160 per night (varies based on season and room type).

Room Amenities:
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Cable TV
  • Tea and coffee-making facilities
  • In-room heating for cooler nights
  • Classic, colonial-style furnishings
On-site Facilities:
  • Kenmare Restaurant: Set in the former with original factory machines, serving Sri Lankan and international cuisine.
  • Spa: Tea-inspired spa treatments including tea baths and massages.
  • Mini Tea Factory Experience: Learn tea processing firsthand, picking and making your own tea.
  • Outdoor Activities: Nature walks, guided tea estate tours, and garden visits.
  • Parking: Complimentary on-site parking available.

Grand Hotel

( No. 5, Grand Hotel Road, Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka)

Originally built in 1891, Grand Hotel is a celebrated luxury property offering old-world charm and modern comfort. Known as one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic colonial hotels, it is perfect for guests looking to enjoy the "Little England" atmosphere of Nuwara Eliya in style and sophistication. Nestled in the lush highlands, Grand Hotel features Tudor-style architecture and beautifully landscaped gardens. Rooms and suites maintain the historic ambiance with period furnishings and modern amenities. The hotel’s dining options include elegant restaurants, a classic tea lounge, and an atmospheric pub. Guests can enjoy leisurely strolls through the gardens, unwind in the spa, or explore nearby attractions like Victoria Park and Horton Plains.

Room Categories:
  • Deluxe Rooms: Elegant rooms with garden or mountain views, ideal for couples or solo travelers.
  • Junior Suites: Spacious suites with separate living areas and views of the surrounding hills.
  • Governor’s Suite: The finest suite, with antique decor, a living room, and exclusive service options.
Rates and Pricing:

Starting from $200 per night (rates vary seasonally and by room type).

Room Amenities:

On-site Facilities:

  • Tea Lounge: Offers traditional high tea in a classic setting with panoramic garden views.
  • Spa: Full-service spa with treatments to rejuvenate after a day of exploring.
  • Fitness Center: Modern fitness facilities with views of the gardens.
  • Outdoor Activities: Golf, horse riding, and guided tours around the town.
