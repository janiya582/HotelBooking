Amangalla

(10 Church Street, Galle 80000, Sri Lanka)

Located in the heart of the historic Galle Fort, Amangalla is a luxury boutique hotel that captures the charm and grandeur of Sri Lanka’s colonial era. With timeless interiors, personalized service, and a relaxing ambiance, it’s ideal for travelers seeking an immersive and historic experience in Galle. Housed in a 17th-century Dutch building, Amangalla combines heritage and luxury. The rooms and suites feature antique furnishings, high ceilings, and spacious verandas with views of the fort and tropical gardens. Guests can enjoy Ayurvedic spa treatments, a beautiful outdoor pool, and fine dining that celebrates Sri Lankan cuisine.

Room Categories:
  • Chambers: Elegant rooms with antique furniture, polished wood floors, and fort views.
  • Suites: Spacious suites with separate living areas, four-poster beds, and garden views.
  • Garden House: A private pavilion with separate dining and living spaces, ideal for families or groups.
Rates and Pricing:

Starting from $400 per night (rates vary based on season, room type, and inclusions).

Room Amenities:
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Air conditioning
  • Luxurious bath amenities
  • Antique furnishings
  • Private verandas (in select rooms)
On-site Facilities:
  • Dining: The Zaal (main dining area) offers traditional Sri Lankan and international cuisine.
  • The Baths Spa: A full-service spa with Ayurvedic treatments, a steam room, and hot stone therapy.
  • Pool: Large outdoor pool surrounded by lush greenery.
  • Library & Lounge: Elegant library and reading lounge with historic books and a relaxing ambiance.
  • Parking: Available for guests.
Policies:

  • Pet Policy: Pets are not allowed.

Fort Bazaar

( 26 Church Street, Galle Fort, Galle, Sri Lanka)

Set within the historic Galle Fort, Fort Bazaar is a chic boutique hotel with a vibrant blend of colonial elegance and modern luxury. Perfectly situated for exploring Galle Fort’s historic charm, this hotel offers a warm and stylish retreat for travelers seeking both relaxation and cultural exploration. Originally an 18th-century townhouse, Fort Bazaar has been thoughtfully restored, blending colonial charm with Moorish-inspired design. The hotel features spacious rooms, a vibrant courtyard, and the popular Church Street Social restaurant. Guests enjoy personalized service, luxurious amenities, and easy access to Galle Fort’s unique shops, cafes, and historic sites.

Room Categories:
  • Bazaar Bedrooms: Cozy, beautifully designed rooms with courtyard or city views.
  • Banyan Bedrooms: Spacious rooms with private terraces overlooking the hotel courtyard.
  • Upper Suites: Luxurious suites with lounge areas, offering expansive views over Galle Fort.
Rates and Pricing:

Starting from $200 per night (rates vary seasonally and by room type).

Room Amenities:
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Air conditioning
  • Smart TVs and Bluetooth speakers
  • Tea and coffee-making facilities
  • Stylish, contemporary decor with local artwork
On-site Facilities:
  • Church Street Social: The hotel’s renowned restaurant offers local and international cuisine.
  • Z Spa: Offers a range of rejuvenating treatments and massages.
  • Courtyard Café & Bar: An outdoor space to enjoy light snacks and cocktails.
  • Library & Lounge: A cozy space to relax, read, and enjoy the hotel’s curated book collection.
  • Parking: Limited parking available; street parking within Galle Fort is common.
Policies:
