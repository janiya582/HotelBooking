Colombo Shangri-La Hotel

(Shangri-La Colombo, 2, 1 Colombo - Galle - Hambantota - Wellawaya Hwy, Colombo 00200)

Located at Colombo’s prestigious One Galle Face, Shangri-La Hotel offers a blend of luxury and serenity with breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean and city skyline. With contemporary interiors, world-class dining, and unmatched hospitality, Shangri-La is ideal for both leisure and business travelers. The hotel offers luxurious accommodations with modern amenities, including spacious rooms and suites that provide a comfortable and elegant stay. Guests can enjoy world-class dining options, a state-of-the-art spa, and easy access to Colombo's cultural landmarks and shopping districts.

Room Categories:
  • Deluxe Room: Elegant rooms with city views, spacious bathrooms, and high-speed Wi-Fi.
  • Premier Ocean Room: A room with panoramic ocean views, complete with a workspace and luxurious amenities.
  • Horizon Club Room: Guests in these rooms enjoy Horizon Club privileges, including access to a private lounge.
  • Special Suites: Luxurious suites with separate living areas, dining spaces, and expansive views of the Indian Ocean.

Galle Face Hotel, Colombo

(2 Galle Road, Colombo 03, Sri Lanka)

Established in 1864, the Galle Face Hotel blends colonial charm with modern comforts. Located along Colombo’s iconic Galle Face Green, the hotel offers historic elegance, luxurious accommodations, and an ideal setting for leisure and business travelers alike. Known for its historic charm and scenic ocean views, the Galle Face Hotel provides guests with a unique experience. The hotel features beautifully appointed rooms, award-winning dining options, and direct access to Colombo's vibrant cultural and shopping scenes.

Room Categories:
  • Landmark Room: Elegant rooms with colonial decor and city views.
  • Ocean View Suite: Spacious suites with stunning views of the Indian Ocean and a private lounge area.
  • Heritage Suite: Historic suites with vintage furnishings and modern amenities for a unique experience.
  • Luxury Suite: Expansive suites with direct sea views, offering the best in luxury and comfort.
