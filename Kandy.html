The Kandy House

(Amunugama Walawwa, Gunnepana, Kandy, Sri Lanka)

A beautiful boutique hotel in the hills of Kandy, The Kandy House offers a unique heritage experience within a restored manor house. Blending luxury with history, this intimate retreat is perfect for travelers seeking tranquility and elegance, with easy access to Kandyâ€™s cultural highlights. Originally built in 1804, The Kandy House is a former ancestral home turned into a boutique hotel. Surrounded by lush gardens and boasting nine uniquely styled rooms, it offers a blend of comfort and heritage charm. Guests can relax by the infinity pool, enjoy traditional Sri Lankan cuisine, and explore the cultural richness of Kandy just a short drive away.

Santani Wellness Resort & Spa

(Arantenna Estate, Werapitiya, Kandy, Sri Lanka)

Nestled in the mountains near Kandy, Santani Wellness Resort & Spa is a luxury wellness retreat designed to rejuvenate mind, body, and soul. Combining sustainable architecture with holistic wellness, the resort offers an eco-friendly sanctuary for relaxation, detox, and wellness experiences. Santani features minimalist yet luxurious accommodations focused on creating a peaceful, distraction-free environment. The resort offers a range of wellness programs, from detox and Ayurveda to yoga and spa therapies, led by experienced wellness professionals. The award-winning design merges with natural surroundings, creating an atmosphere that encourages healing and relaxation in harmony with nature.

Room Categories:
  • Mountain View Chalets: Spacious, open-air rooms with stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Garden View Chalets: Located closer to nature, these rooms offer peaceful garden views and a secluded ambiance.
  • Santani Villa: A private villa offering luxurious accommodations, including private facilities, living spaces, and panoramic mountain views.
Rates and Pricing:

Starting from $350 per night (includes wellness consultations; rates may vary seasonally and by package).

Room Amenities:
