98 Acres Resort & Spa

( 98 Acres Resort, Uva Province, Ella, Sri Lanka)

Nestled in the verdant hills of Ella, 98 Acres Resort & Spa is a luxury eco-resort set on a working tea estate. Known for its breathtaking views and sustainable design, the resort offers a tranquil escape for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. Set on a 98-acre tea estate, the resort’s accommodations blend natural elements with luxury, offering guests a unique eco-friendly retreat. Guests can explore nearby attractions like Little Adam’s Peak and Nine Arches Bridge, or unwind with a spa treatment surrounded by panoramic mountain views. The resort emphasizes sustainable tourism, using eco-conscious materials and practices that reflect the natural beauty of its surroundings.

Room Categories:
  • Standard Rooms: Cozy rooms with natural stone walls, hardwood floors, and views of the tea estate.
  • Deluxe Rooms: Spacious rooms featuring private balconies with panoramic mountain and garden views.
  • Grand Executive Suites: Luxurious suites with a living area, jacuzzi, and sweeping views of Ella’s mountain ranges.

EKHO Ella

(EKHO Ella, Ella, Uva Province, Sri Lanka)

Nestled in the heart of Ella, EKHO Ella offers stunning views of Ella Gap, modern comfort, and Sri Lankan hospitality. This boutique hotel is ideal for travelers seeking a blend of luxury, scenic beauty, and close proximity to Ella’s famous attractions. EKHO Ella combines contemporary design with local influences, offering spacious rooms and suites that provide a front-row seat to the stunning Ella Gap. The on-site restaurant serves local and international cuisine, while an outdoor terrace provides a perfect spot to enjoy Ella’s famous sunsets. Centrally located, EKHO Ella is close to popular sights like Little Adam’s Peak and Nine Arches Bridge, making it an ideal base for exploration.
